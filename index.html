lazySocialButtons

A JavaScript plugin to place social buttons on a page on user interaction (mouseover) to spare the initial page load from the 300kb+ download requests for social APIs.

    lazySocialButtons.js

    Social buttons are great ways to spread the word about your site or blog, and the popular social sites provide easy snippets of code to let you add them to your page without a lot of fuss. The problem is that these scripts can add a lot of weight to your page. All of their resources must load on the page before your visitor can interact with it, and depending on where you place them, can even delay the visual parts of the page from loading until they are finished.

    The lazySocialButtons plugin solves this problem by only loading the social buttons on your page when your visitor interacts with them, specifically when they mouse over them for the first time. You still get the benefits of social networking without making your visitors wait to see your site.
